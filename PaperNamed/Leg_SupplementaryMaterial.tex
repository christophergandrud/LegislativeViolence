\subsection*{Details on Prior Correction of the Rare Logistic Regression Models}

For prior correction \citep[see][]{KingRareEventsPA2001} in the models with the full sample of elected multi-party legislatures I used the observed proportion of all observations with legislative violence up to 2010: i.e. 2.1 percent of observations up until 2010 had violence ($\tau = \frac{71}{3370} = 0.021$). There were 63 observed incidences of violence and 2654 country-years from 1990 through 2009 in the sample, so: $\tau = \frac{63}{2654} = 0.024$.
